
	Veejay processes nativly in YUV, hence no RGB <-> YUV conversions are used

		YUV 4:2:0 Planar ( 1 Cr and 1 Cb sample per 2x2 Y samples)
		YUV 4:2:2 Planar ( 1 Cr and 1 Cr sample per 1x2 Y samples)  

	You can customize the sampling method used when sub- and super sampling
	video data:    
		OSC: /config/sampling
		VIMS: 250:;

	Use the triangle filter for best quality, or the box filter for best performance		(default is best quality).
